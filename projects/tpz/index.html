Maker-in-Residence Projects at The Possible Zone

march 20, 2023 -- may 26, 2023

During the spring of 2023, I visited The Possible Zone in Boston, MA and spent time as a Maker-in-Residence. I helped wake up and document machines in their wonderful new FabLab, assisted students with fabrication projects, and built a series of example projects which highlighted the capabilities of the space.
