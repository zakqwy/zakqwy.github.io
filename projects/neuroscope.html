NeuroScope


I spent a weekend building a Teensy 3.2-based prototype membrane potential visualizer for the NeuroBytes project. It ended up being one of the most important prototypes I’ve ever built – we got our best response from our customers, and its technical requirements drove our development efforts for the next few years.
